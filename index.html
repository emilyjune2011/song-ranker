Song ranker

Pick the better song until your list is sorted. Works with Spotify links.

1. Connect Spotify

If this copy is hosted, the owner sets it up once; you only need your own Spotify account. Click Sign in with Spotify. If you are the one deploying, add redirect URI in the Spotify Developer Dashboard for your app (must match this page’s address exactly).

Local / no hosting: create an app in the dashboard, add the redirect URI above, paste your Client ID here and save — or put it in config.js if you deploy.

2. Load songs

Paste a Spotify link to a playlist, artist, or album (open in Spotify → Share → Copy link).

Or add tracks by hand

One line per song: Artist – Title | https://open.spotify.com/track/…
